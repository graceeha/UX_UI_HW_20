We began our research by first conducting a competitor analysis and heuristic evaluation of the website. Just by reviewing the website alone gave us many ideas as to what areas we can improve greatly on. At first glance, it’s quite obvious that the website had not been updated in years and the designer at the time probably didn’t take the time to look at it from a user’s point of view.
